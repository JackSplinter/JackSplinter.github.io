Solve you own board or try sample board 16x16.
The game is to connect all of the islands (circles) with each other using bridges (lines). Each one of the bridges connect precisely two islands and they can't cross, between two islands can be two bridges at most. Digit placed on the island means how many bridges connect this island with others. Every island must have got at least one bridge connecting it to other island, so it is possible to reach each island from any other.
